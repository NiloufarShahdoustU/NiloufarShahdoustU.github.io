I am a PhD candidate in Electrical and Computer Engineering at the University of Utah. My research sits at the intersection of neuroscience and reinforcement learning with a focus on how neural activity supports decision-making under risk and cognitive control. I work with intracranial recordings including LFP, and single-neuron data, and I build computational models that connect neural measurements to behavior.
